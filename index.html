About

Born on June 20th 2001, Britney would consider herself an introvert.
In 2015, Britney enrolled in a 9 th grade summer program called Sport and Arts in School Foundation, SASF , that gave her the opportunity to make friends, and get close to the staff of the school. She spent her high school career happily devoted to SASF, where she became a volunteer and later on an intern.
